Software


Cura Slicer

This is the software that is used to translate your 3D models into something that the 3D printer can print with. It does require a survey before you can download it, it is Free and it does NOT need an email address


Videos


Maker's Muse 3D Printing 101 Playlist

This playlist provided a lof of useful knowledge to me when I began 3D printing. I initially began with 'What should you print first', '10 places to get free 3d printing files', and 'The best way to orient your prints' videos. After those I suggest watching any video that you feel is important to you. Such as if you want to make pieces that can withstand a bit more abuse, watch the '3D print stronger parts' video.
